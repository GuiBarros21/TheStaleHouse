About Us

Restaurant style

With a nod to classic European bistros, our focus is on seasonal food, paired well. We make a point of buying the best ingredients from local farmers, and allowing each dish’s individual flavors to speak for themselves. The menu is designed for sharing, with a focus on small and medium sized plates. Our charcuterie boards are garnished with seasonal condiments made here in house, with an eye toward pairing with the perfect glass of wine. We are open for Lunch Tuesday – Friday, and Brunch on the weekend.

About us

Since the turn of the century South Granville has been an important hub for the city, bridging downtown to the west side and boasting some of the best shops and galleries in Vancouver. In recent years not only has it become a destination, but also a vibrant urban village supported by all the people who live and work here. The Stable House Bistro was formed by a group of locals to celebrate this history and sense of community by creating a meeting place to share good food, wine and time together. Named “The Stable House” to pay homage to the structures that scattered the area over 100 years ago, we are proud of what this neighborhood was and is—and hope to be a part of its future for many years to come.

Wine

Here at the Stable House, we think of wine as a grocery, not a luxury. We want to take back the world of wine from the stuffy 1%ers and share it with our customers in an environment free of pretension and artifice. Our eclectic list is curated by Matthew Landry, one of BC’s top sommeliers, and prizes the humble everyday wines of Europe above all others. We currently offer more than sixteen wines by the glass, as well as a deeper reserve list for the avid wine geek (our favourite kind of person). Do note, our wines change almost daily, so we can’t guarantee your favourite bottle will be here when you return. Such is life.
